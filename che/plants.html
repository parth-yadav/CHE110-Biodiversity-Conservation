Baobab Tree

The Baobab tree, scientifically known as Adansonia
digitata, is a distinctive African tree found in India's
Gujarat region. Known forits massive, bulbous trunk and
drought-resistant nature, it plays a crucial role in local
ecosystems

Rhodendron

The Rhododendron tree, native to India's Himalayan
region features vibrant red or pink flowers. It's
celebrated for its natural beauty and cultural
significance, often used in traditional ceremonies
and known for its hardy, cold-resistant nature.

Neem tree

The Neem tree (Azadirachta indica) is a revered
Indian plant known for its medicinal properties.
Its leaves, seeds, and oil are used in
traditional remedies, and it serves as
a natural insect repellent.

Peepal Tree

The Peepal tree (Ficus religiosa)
is sacred in Hinduism. It has
heart-shaped leaves and aerial
roots, symbolizing spiritual
enlightenment. Often found near
temples, it holds cultural and
religious significance in India.

Tulsi Plant

Tulsi, or Holy Basil (Ocimum sanctum), is a
revered herb in India. Its aromatic leaves
are used in traditional medicine and religious
rituals. Known for its medicinal properties,
it's considered sacred and symbolizes purity
and protection.

Teak tree

The Teak tree (Tectona grandis) is a tropical
hardwood native to India. Renowned for its
durable and termite-resistant wood, it's
valuable for furniture and construction.
With a straight trunk, it's prized for
its timber quality.

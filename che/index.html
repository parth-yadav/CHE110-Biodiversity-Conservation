What is Biodiversity?

Biodiversity, short for biological diversity, refers to the wide variety of life on Earth. It encompasses
the numerous species of plants, animals, microorganisms, and their unique ecosystems. Biodiversity is
essential for the health of our planet and plays a crucial role in sustaining life.

Biodiversity Conservation

Biodiversity conservation is the practice of protecting and preserving the diversity of life on Earth.
It involves various strategies and efforts to prevent the loss of species and ecosystems. Conservation
methods include establishing protected areas, sustainable resource management, habitat restoration, and
raising awareness about the importance of biodiversity. Conservation efforts are vital to ensure the long-term
health of our planet and the well-being of future generations.

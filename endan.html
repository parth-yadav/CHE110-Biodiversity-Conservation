Siberian Tiger

The Siberian tiger, a magnificent
and critically endangered large feline
found in Russia and China, faces
severe threats from habitat loss and
illegal hunting. Urgent conservation
measures are essential to safeguard
this impressive species from extinction.

Snow leopard

The snow leopard is an endangered
big cat inhabiting mountainous regions
of Central and South Asia. Its population
is declining due to poaching and
habitat loss, making conservation efforts
crucial for its survival.

Red panda

The red panda is an endangered
small mammal native to the Himalayas
and southwestern China. Its population
is declining due to habitat loss and poaching,
necessitating conservation efforts to protect
this unique and charming species..

Blue Whale

The blue whale, the largest animal
on Earth, is an endangered
marine mammal threatened
by ship strikes and entanglement
in fishing gear,emphasizing the need
for protective measures to ensure its survival.

Javan rhinoceros

The Javan rhinoceros is a critically
endangered species of rhinoceros found
in Southeast Asia,with only a handful of
individuals remaining. Intense poaching and
habitat loss have pushed this species to the
brink of extinction, necessitating
urgent conservation efforts.

Thylacine

The thylacine, also known as the
Tasmanian tiger, is an extinct carnivorous
marsupial native to Tasmania. It is listed
as endangered, but it is important to note
that it is considered extinct, as there is no
confirmed evidence of its existence in the
wild since the 1930s.
